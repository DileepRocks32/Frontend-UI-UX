GATE College, Tirupati: Courses, Fee Structure, Eligibility ..
GATE College, Tirupati was established in 1998 under the aegis of GATE Educational Trust.
It is approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi and is affiliated to Sri Venkateswara University.
GATE College aims at training young people to be quality leaders to bring the desired changes for the betterment of the people of our country. The College trains young minds to serve their fellowmen in justice, truth and love.
It fosters an atmosphere of intellectual vigor and moral rectitude in which the youth of our country may find their fulfillment and achieve greatness as eminent persons of service.
